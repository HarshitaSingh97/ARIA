Angie

Breed: Labrdor       Age: 10 years      
Genger: Female

Hi friend! I am Angie, a gorgeous and incredibly gentle 10 year old yellow lab. I am a fabulous family dog! I am great with other dogs, cat, and kids of all ages. I'm a typically happy go lucky lab that adores everyone I meet. I am housebroken and trustworthy in the house, but I do have one BIG fear. I am absolutely terrified of thunder storms. I need medication to calm my fears and will need a family that understands and is willing to commit to working with my fear for life. Fireworks, on the other hand, don't really bother me. Just storms. I am a healthy girl, however I just finished treatment for heartworm, so if you have any questions, please just ask the rescue! I really hope to find a forever home soon!.
