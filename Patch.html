Patch

Breed: Lab Mix       Age:9 years
Gender: Male

Hi there! I'm Patch, a very sweet little guy who is so happy to have a second chance! I am friendly with other dogs and cats, and I love to snuggle, but only once I get to know you. I am an adorable long-haired chihuahua mix. Like many chihuahuas, I know what I want out of life and can be a bit bossy about it, so a home without kids is a must for me. I would be absolutely fine with another laid back dog, and I'm totally okay with kitties. When I want attention, I'll nudge you and wiggle across your lap. I also like to sit under your desk at your feet when you're working or sit beside you on the couch. I'd love to find a home of my own, one that loves fluffy chihuahua mixes like me!
