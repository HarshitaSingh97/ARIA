Blanca

Breed: Westie/Maltese       Age:8 years
Gender: Female

Hi there! I'm Blanca, and I am a pretty pretty princess! I am an 8-year-old Westese, or a Westie/Maltese mix. I absolutely adore my people, and love love love to show them how much I love them. I would be the absolute perfect companion for an older adult or kid-free home where I can rule the roost - just me, and no other four-legged siblings. Grandkids can visit, but please just give me my own space to feel safe while they are over. I am housebroken and trustworthy in the house, and enjoy a good snooze in a comfy bed when you're gone. I love to play, and will entertain myself with soft squeaky toys or play with you. I love going for walks, but no need for dog parks or doggy socials for me - I prefer to hang with humans only. My favorite past time is hanging out with you, no matter what we are doing. I'm longing for someone to keep company, and to keep me company. Do you know a human who could use a good friend to talk to and love? I'm here and waiting!
