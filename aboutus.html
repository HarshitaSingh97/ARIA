About Us

"Our mission, is to rescue. Our dream, is that one day, we won't have to!"



A.R.I.A. is a website founded to help all kinds of dogs, cats and other animals in need of help, find good homes.

Our Journey first began when we gave our home to two pups from the street, whom we lovingly call Prince and Tiger. It was then, that the idea of A.R.I.A. surfaced, sparked by our keen interest in the betterment of the lives of these unfortunate animals who are left to fend for themselves on the streets. A.R.I.A yearns to find caring homes for many more pups like Tiger and Prince, and many more such animals waiting to be welcomed into their new lives.



It is hard to see them arrive
whether they're battered,abandoned or starving.
Sometimes, it is just as hard to see them leave,
even to the best of homes.
A rescuer's most selfless quality
is to make room to save another,
regardless of how it hurts to say goodbye!




A.R.I.A began in August,2016 to provide support for a worthy cause through easy, online actions. Since its launch, A.R.I.A. has contributed to help animals around the country. Our website thrives to provide shelter, food and the much deserved love to all unfortunate yet, deserving animals. All donations generated by A.R.I.A activities are used to make sure all animals are fed well, provided shelter, well vaccinated, fit, healthy and satisfied.
