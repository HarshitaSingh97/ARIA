A.R.I.A.

Connecting homeless pets with loving forever homes

We believe there is a home for every pet in need and enough love to save the lives of all homeless pets in pounds and shelters. We’ve made it our mission to put homeless pets into the hearts and minds of people in such a powerful way that killing adoptable animals becomes completely unacceptable in India.

Why India’s homeless pets urgently need our help

According to the statistics of 2015, about 1,920 animal cruelty cases are reported every year. Our mission at A.R.I.A is to take in sick and injured animals, assist them with their rehabilitation, and find them loving homes.
We cannot do this alone…we need your help. Our work is funded entirely by people just like you who love animals and do everything in their power to make sure that homeless, neglected, sick and injured animals get the care they need. Every donation that is made towards this initiative will be used to protect and feed these innocent beings and raise awareness towards animal abuse .
The Government's system places little or no value on pet adoption, leaving it to community groups and charities to take responsibility for saving the lives of pets that remain unclaimed at the pound.
