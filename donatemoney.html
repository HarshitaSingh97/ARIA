Donate

Animals Need Your Help

With your gift, you are helping animals around the world. By supporting A.R.I.A., you protect endangered animals, provide emergency relief to animals in crises, and rescue and protect dogs, cats, rabbits and other animals.

Ways To Donate

  • Cheque   favouring 'A.R.I.A. Bangalore'which can be couriered to our office address (Kensington Apts, Flat D-GF, Ulsoor Cross Road, Off Bazaar Street, Ulsoor, Bangalore, 560 008)

  • Online payment gateway   If transferring your donation online, please send a screenshot of the transaction to aria@gmail.com so that we can track the transfer immediately

  • In cash   at any of our listed centres! Please remember to take your receipt immediately.

We need your financial support in order to put a stop to cruelty to animals.

Together, we can make the world a better place for all beings. Your donation will go to work instantly to help
animals who are suffering and dying in vivisection laboratories, on factory farms, in zoos and circuses and in the
leather and fur industries.
